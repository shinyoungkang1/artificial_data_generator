# Retail Receipt OCR Notes

## Document shape

- Store header and cashier/terminal metadata
- Itemized line entries with qty, unit price, line total
- Tax, subtotal, total, payment method

## OCR-targeted mess

- receipt skew and perspective-like rotation
- blur and low contrast on thermal-like text
- random speckles and darker edge gradients
- inconsistent spacing and punctuation in SKUs/prices
